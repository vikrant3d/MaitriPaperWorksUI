About us


We are specialist in making eco friendly disposable consumer products. Our products includes Thali (Patravali), Plates, Dish, Drones, Glass, Tissue papers & Spoons. Our specialty is all our products are eco-friendly and made of paper which are bio degradable.
Maitri itself defines friendship. So our aim is to friendship with the world through our products. Maitri Paperworks is the MAKE IN INDIA initiative and was established in year 2018. We are specialist in making eco friendly consumer products. Our vision is to give excellent quality products to the India. Our mission is to deliver the products to every corner of the Nation. As we are growing everyday new consumers are adding in our Maitri family. Hope our efforts add some value to MAKE IN INDIA initiative.
