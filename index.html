About us


   Maitri itself defines friendship. Our aim is to spread our innovations in paper product industry. Maitri Paperworks is MAKE IN INDIA initiative and was established in year 2018. We are expert in making eco friendly disposable consumable products. Our products line includes Thali (Patravali), Plates, Dish, Drones, Glass, Tissue papers Spoons and more.
   All our products are bio degradable thus are does no harm to environment. Our vision is to provide excellent quality products to the World. As we are growing new milestones are added to Maitri Paperworks. Hope our efforts add some value to our nation.
